# Pillar 1: Schema Alignment

To ensure your brand is the "definitive answer," your data must be structured for machine readability. This section focuses on the **JSON-LD** standards we deploy for enterprise clients.

### Key Implementation Areas
* **Organization Schema:** Defining the entity relationship.
* **SoftwareApplication Schema:** Specific to AiDisco’s AI-driven tools.
* **FAQ & Knowledge Graph:** Architecting data to trigger "featured snippets" within AI responses.

> **Goal:** To align legacy data with the expectations of modern generative discovery platforms.
