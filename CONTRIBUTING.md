# Contributing to the AiDisco AEO Framework

Thank you for your interest in contributing to the future of **Answer Engine Optimization (AEO)**. We believe the shift from traditional search to generative discovery is a collaborative frontier[cite: 1]. 

By contributing to this repository, you help define the standards for how brands and data are perceived by AI models like Gemini, ChatGPT, and Perplexity[cite: 1].

---

## How to Contribute

### 1. Propose Schema Improvements
If you have identified new **Schema.org** types or properties that improve AI "readability" and entity recognition, please submit a proposal[cite: 1]. 
*   Ensure your schema examples are in valid **JSON-LD**[cite: 1].
*   Provide a brief explanation of how this alignment helps LLMs better categorize enterprise data[cite: 1].

### 2. Refine Prompt Architectures
We welcome contributions to our library of strategic prompts[cite: 1]. 
*   Prompts should focus on **verifiability** and **latent space visibility**[cite: 1].
*   Submissions should include the intended platform (e.g., specific to Claude, Gemini, or general LLM behavior)[cite: 1].

### 3. Expand Authority Signals
Share methods or directory integrations that serve as strong signals for AI citation engines[cite: 1].
*   Help us update our **Authority-Signal-Directories List.xlsx** with new high-impact platforms[cite: 1].

---

## Submission Process

1.  **Fork the Repository:** Create your own branch from the `main` branch[cite: 1].
2.  **Follow the Format:** Use the existing `.md` templates for Schema Alignment and Prompt Architecture[cite: 1].
3.  **Submit a Pull Request (PR):** 
    *   Provide a clear title and description of your changes[cite: 1].
    *   Reference any related issues or specific AI models your contribution addresses[cite: 1].
4.  **Review:** The AiDisco team will review your PR for alignment with our core pillars[cite: 1].
