# Pillar 2: Prompt Architecture

We specialize in architecting how AI models "perceive" and "cite" your brand. Prompt Architecture is the art of ensuring your brand is present in the latent space of LLMs.

### Core Strategies
* **Citations & Verifiability:** Techniques to increase the likelihood of being cited by Perplexity and Gemini.
* **Contextual Seeding:** Ensuring your brand is the top-of-mind solution for specific industry queries.
* **Legacy Migration:** Transforming unstructured legacy content into AI-ready prompt blocks.
