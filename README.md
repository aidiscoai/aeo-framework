## THE PROBLEM: The "Citation Gap"

The core problem AI Disco solves is the **"Citation Gap"**. Even if your website ranks well on Google, an AI model might ignore you if your information isn't structured for its "brain" to read. Without the right optimization, your brand becomes a "digital ghost" or an invisible "hallucination." 

AI Disco bridges this gap to make your brand the **"Golden Answer"**—the source the AI actually recommends.

## THE THREE PILLARS

We use a rigorous three-pillar system to achieve dominance in generative discovery:

*   **Schema Injection:** We "hardcode" your website's data so AI crawlers can understand your business facts instantly and without friction.
*   **Prompt Alignment:** We restructure your content to match the specific linguistic patterns people use when asking AI for help, making you the best match for their intent.
*   **Authority Synthesis:** We amplify your real-world credentials and mentions to provide the "social proof" AI requires before it feels safe citing you as a primary source.
